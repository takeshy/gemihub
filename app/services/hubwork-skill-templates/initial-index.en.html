What is the Webpage Builder skill?

A built-in AI skill that turns conversation into a working site. You describe what you want — a login flow, a contact form, a booking page, a blog — and the assistant produces the HTML, the API workflows, the auth wiring, and the spreadsheet schema, all under web/ on your Drive.

  • ✓ Public pages and protected pages with magic-link login
  • ✓ JSON API endpoints backed by Google Sheets
  • ✓ Contact forms, bookings, calendar integration
  • ✓ Admin pages for cancel / reply / no-show flows
  • ✓ Blog and announcement publishing from Drive notes
  • ✓ Auto schema migration when you add new sheets
